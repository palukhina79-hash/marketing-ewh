## 1) Methodology (short)
**Inclusion (“Main players”) filter applied:**
1) Confirmed presence in **≥2 MEA markets** *or* strong verified presence in **one** key market (e.g., KSA, UAE, South Africa).  
2) Systemic digital footprint (site + at least 1 corporate social presence; regional socials often **DATA GAP**).  
3) Evidence of water-heater portfolio promoted/sold in MEA (official catalog pages and/or retailer/distributor listings).  
4) At least one confirmed channel (retail / marketplace / distributor / projects) with URL proof.

**Product typing tags used (only if evidenced):**
- **ESWH** = Electric storage  
- **EIH** = Electric instant/tankless  
- **GWH** = Gas  
- **SWH** = Solar  
- **HPWH** = Heat pump  
- **COMM** = Commercial/industrial

**Price positioning method (standardized):**
- Anchor: **~50L electric storage** (or closest equivalent if 50L ESWH not evidenced in MEA → explicitly marked).
- Evidence: **3–7 public price observations** in MEA where available; otherwise **DATA GAP** with “how to obtain”.
- FX: For pegged currencies, USD conversion uses official peg documentation:
  - **AED/USD 3.6725** (Central Bank of UAE): https://www.centralbank.ae/en/our-operations/monetary-operations/domestic-market-operations/  
  - **SAR/USD 3.75** (SAMA / IMF references): https://www.sama.gov.sa/en-US/Pages/default.aspx and IMF background note: https://www.imf.org/external/np/fin/ert/GUI/Pages/Background.aspx
